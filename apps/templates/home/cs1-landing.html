Clinical Scenario

You are working on the medicine inpatient floor. You are about to have an encounter with a 22 year female patient who you have never seen before, but are here following an overdose from opioids. You read in their chart that they have no past history. The nurse also noted that the patient’s best friend at the bedside commented that “she has a long history of trauma since she was 22 years old”.

To start, we are practicing carrying out a trauma-informed introduction with a variety of patient states. First, the patient will respond neutrally. Next, she will respond sadly. Finally, she will respond with anger, being dissatisfied with the treatment. Your goal is to respond accordingly.
